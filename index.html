About Me

I am Farhan Tanvir Utshaw, a Graduate Assistant in the Department of Computer Science at Missouri State University. Previously, I was a Lecturer at United International University (CSE Department) and the Android Team Lead at BYDO Academy. With 2.5+ years of professional experience, I have worked across front-end, back-end, and mobile development.

Healthcare platform

Uposorgo is a tech based Healthcare Start Up which aims to bring world class healthcare services to people's doorsteps. It is an online doctor appointment service platform which is integrated with many other services. Uposorgo provides a real time doctor's consultation through a user friendly app interface and fully secured communication channel between doctors and patients. The vision and mission of Uposorgo is to connect the root level people even from the remote areas so that they can avail standard healthcare services which will bring positive impact in their lives. Uposorgo believes in continuous development in healthcare services keeping pace with the advancement of technology. Uposorgo is committed to connecting and bringing people from all the spectrum of society in an online umbrella healthcare system with quality services as part of social responsibility.
